About Us

we're revolutionizing the way students approach learning and note-taking. Our AI-powered platform enhances your study materials, turning ordinary notes into comprehensive learning resources.

Founded in 2025, our team of education specialists, developers, and AI experts from IUT have been working to create tools that make studying more efficient and effective. We believe that technology should serve education, not replace the human element of learning.

Our mission is to empower students worldwide by providing accessible, intelligent tools that adapt to individual learning styles and needs. Join us on this journey to transform education and unlock your full potential.
